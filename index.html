you're never too far from home

An app for the homesick. BRIDGE provides a side by side comparison between where you currently are and where you’ve come from. Compare surroundings and stay up to date with the places their hearts yearn for. Connect with your friends and see how their day is going, too. BRIDGE keeps you connected to the people and places that matter most.

Who needs BRIDGE?

We're all adults, but everyone longs for home every now and then.

College Kids

Leaving home for the first time is intense. Keep in touch with the town you left behind.

Families

Sometimes family members move for work, school, or love. Connect to their daily life.

Professionals

You can simultaniously love business trips and home. Know what's going on while you're gone.
